# Genesis

## Abraham - God's Friend (Genesis 18)

When God said He would destroy Sodom and Gomorrah, Abraham questioned Him:

> "Should not the Judge of all the earth do what is right?"

God did not punish Abraham for questioning. God made Abraham the model of friendship with God throughout Scripture. Abraham is called God's friend—something that cannot apply to someone who merely obeys without understanding.

**What this shows:**
- God invites honest questions from His friends
- True relationship is friendship, not blind obedience
- God welcomes being reverently questioned about His justice
