## What it looks and feels like

- Serif body type, sans-serif headings, generous whitespace
- Light/dark toggle
- Reads beautifully on phone and desktop
- Looks like a serious publisher's site, not a blog
- Restrained — the writing is the star

Two clear elements:

2. **Below:** four or five cards linking into the main sections (Study, Essays, Books, Videos, Events). Sections with no content yet say "in progress" — honest, not hidden.
